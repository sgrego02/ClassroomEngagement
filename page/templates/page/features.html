Features

Check out all the tools you can use.

Buttons

Students can press a button if they don't understand something about the lecture, if they want the lecturer to slow down or if they want the lecturer to give an example. Lecturers can see the amount of students who pressed any button in the form of a bar chart.

Question

Lecturers can ask a question with multiple choice answers to check students' understanding. Both students and lecturers can see the results when the lecturer presses Get Results button.

Students' questions

Students can ask a question about the lecture. Lecturers can see all the questions that the students asked during the lecture.

Feedback

Students can send feedback about the lecture. Lecturers can see all the feedback that the students sent about the lecture.
